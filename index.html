Welcome to the official website of Noah Zacharin

In a review of 'crow dark wind', Tom Semioli of Minor 7th said:

"like Bruce Cockburn, Ry Cooder, and Bonnie Raitt, Noah Zacharin possesses a virtuoso command of the guitar, world-wise songwriting". 

Dubbed "Big Daddy Z" by his fans, he has also been called: 

"deserving to be mentioned in the same breath with names such as James Taylor and David Wilcox"(Minor 7th, review of 'aLIVE!')

"a stunning guitarist...no stylistic boundaries"(Holger Peterson, Stony Plain Records, CBC, CKUA)

"one of the best on the scene... the leader of his community"(Danny Marks, Blues FM)

"un excellent guitariste"(Dan Behrman, Radio Canada)

"a wonderful songwriter and performer"(Paul Mills, Borealis Records)

"one of the best songwriters this country has produced"(Rick Fielding)

"a poet, a songwriter, a singer we have been waiting for"(Penny Lang)

"an extraordinary poet and music maker"(Lori Cullen)
